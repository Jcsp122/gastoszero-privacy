GastosZero Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

GastosZero is designed as a local-first personal finance tracker. The core app does not require an account, login, social sign-in, cloud sync, ads, or a remote financial database.

This Privacy Policy explains how GastosZero handles user and device data. It applies to the GastosZero mobile app and related app services provided by John Carlo Perez, the developer of GastosZero.

Information You Enter in the App

GastosZero lets you enter and manage personal finance information, such as:

  • Preferred name or nickname used inside the app.
  • Budget amount, budget frequency, currency, and app preferences.
  • Expense and income logs, including amount, category, note, date, and wallet.
  • Wallet names, wallet types, balances, transfers, and balance history.
  • Recurring payments, debts, due dates, savings goals, and progress.
  • Local app settings such as theme, language, color-blind mode, notification setting, and app-lock choices.

This information is stored locally on your device or browser storage. GastosZero does not require you to create an account and does not upload your core finance records to a cloud database.

Optional CSV Export

You can choose to export selected finance data as a CSV file for backup, review, or spreadsheet use. CSV export is user-initiated. Once exported, the file is controlled by you and may be accessible to apps, cloud storage, or people you choose to share it with outside GastosZero.

Optional AI Assistant

GastosZero may include an optional AI assistant or command parser. When you use the online AI mode, the message you type and a limited app context needed to answer your request may be sent to the configured GastosZero backend and to the AI service provider used by that backend, such as OpenAI.

The AI assistant is used to draft finance entries, answer budget questions, or help parse commands. AI-generated changes are treated as drafts and must be confirmed by you before they are saved in the app. If you use only local app features or local AI mode, the core finance tracker can be used without sending finance data to the online AI service.

Device Features and Permissions

GastosZero may use the following device features:

  • Internet access: used to load app resources and, if enabled, contact the optional online AI backend.
  • Notifications: used for app reminders when you enable notification features and grant permission on your device.
  • Device unlock: used for optional app-lock features. GastosZero checks whether device unlock is available, but it does not receive your fingerprint, face, or biometric template.

How We Use Information

GastosZero uses app data to provide app functionality, including:

  • Showing budgets, wallets, spending summaries, charts, debts, and goals.
  • Saving your local preferences and local app state.
  • Creating user-requested CSV exports.
  • Scheduling user-enabled reminders and notifications.
  • Providing optional AI command parsing or coaching when you choose to use online AI mode.

Sharing and Sale of Data

GastosZero does not sell your personal or sensitive user data. The app does not include advertising SDKs.

GastosZero may share limited data only in these situations:

  • With service providers that help provide an optional feature you use, such as a hosted backend or AI service provider for online AI mode.
  • When you choose to export or share your own data, such as by creating a CSV file.
  • When required to comply with applicable law, legal process, or a valid government request.

Security

GastosZero is designed to keep core finance data on your device. Online AI requests, when enabled, should be sent over HTTPS to the configured backend. API keys for AI services are kept on the backend and should not be bundled into the mobile app.

No method of storage or transmission is completely secure. You should protect your device, lock screen, backups, and any exported CSV files.

Retention and Deletion

Local app data remains on your device until you delete it, reset the app, uninstall the app, or clear the app/browser storage. GastosZero includes a Reset Data option that deletes local finance data and returns the app to setup.

Exported CSV files are not deleted by resetting GastosZero because those files are controlled by your device, file manager, spreadsheet app, or any service where you saved them.

Children

GastosZero is not directed to children under 13. Do not use GastosZero to knowingly submit personal information about children.
